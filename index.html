Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Your privacy matters. Read on to understand how MindBloom protects your data.

1. Introduction

MindBloom ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using MindBloom, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you first set up the App:

  • Name: Your full name (stored locally on your device)
  • Age: Your age (stored locally on your device)

2.2 Activity Data

As you use the App, we collect:

  • Task Hours: Number of hours spent on daily activities
  • Task Remarks: Your notes and comments about each activity
  • Screen Time: Total daily screen time automatically fetched from your device
  • Mental Health Status: Status indicators (Green/Yellow/Red) based on screen time
  • Challenge Progress: Your current day, completion status, and streak history

2.3 Technical Logs

For quality improvement, we collect:

  • Action Logs: Records of app actions (opening settings, submitting entries)
  • Error Logs: Any errors that occur during app usage
  • Timestamps: When specific actions were performed

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  1. Providing Core Functionality - Track your 45-day challenge and store activity records
  2. Improving the App - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  3. User Experience - Display personalized dashboards and statistics
  4. Data Persistence - Save your challenge data across app sessions

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage Only

🔒 All data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT send data to external servers, use cloud storage, or share with third parties.

4.2 Storage Format

  • Data is stored in a local SQLite database: discipline_challenge.db
  • Configuration is stored in: app_config.json
  • All storage is encrypted by your device's security system

4.3 Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you uninstall the App
  • You can reset all data using the "Reset Challenge" feature
  • Uninstalling the App removes all associated data

5. Screen Time Access

5.1 Automatic Fetching

  • The App automatically reads your device's screen time data daily
  • Data is fetched directly from your device's built-in usage statistics
  • Only total daily hours are stored, not detailed app usage

5.2 Your Control

You can revoke screen time permission anytime in your device's Settings → Apps → MindBloom → Permissions

6. What We Do NOT Collect

Your email address or phone number
Your location or GPS data
Details about other apps you use
Browsing history or internet usage
Biometric data (fingerprints, face recognition)
Device identifiers or personal information
Analytics or tracking cookies

7. Third-Party Services

MindBloom does NOT integrate with any external services:

  • ❌ Google Analytics or analytics services
  • ❌ Advertisement networks
  • ❌ Social media platforms
  • ❌ Cloud storage services
  • ❌ Backend servers
  • ❌ Payment processors

The App is entirely self-contained and operates offline.

8. Data Security

8.1 Protection Measures

  • Data is stored in a local SQLite database with restricted access
  • Your device's OS provides encryption for the database file
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No user accounts or passwords required

9. Your Rights and Choices

9.1 Data Access & Deletion

Delete individual task entries before submission
Reset all data using the "Reset Challenge" button
Uninstall the App to remove all data

9.2 Permissions Control

Revoke screen time permission anytime in device Settings. The App will continue to function with reduced features.
